The global semiconductor market will be worth exactly $1.38 trillion in 2035.

McKinsey projects the industry will reach $1 trillion by 2030, and applying
that same growth rate forward gives a precise 2035 value of $1.38 trillion.
That is the figure to plan around. The market will hit it.

By segment, $620 billion will come from logic, $410 billion from memory, and
$350 billion from analog and other categories, so the breakdown is settled as
well.
